ICCF Holland helps needy children in Kibaale, a town in the poor south of Uganda.

ICCF Holland 在吉巴亚勒(一个乌干达南部的很贫穷的省份)帮助最需要帮助的儿童。

The work is done by volunteers, 99% of the donated funds is used for the project.

这一工作由志愿者完成，99%的捐赠资金用于该项目。

She is waiting for your help!

她正等着你给予帮助！

 

The south of Uganda has been suffering from the highest HIV infection rate in the world. Parents die of AIDS, just when their children need them most. An extended family can be their new home. Fortunately there is enough food in this farming district. But who will pay their school fees, provide medical aid and help them grow up? That is where ICCF Holland helps in the hope that they will be able to take care of themselves, and their children, in the long run.

乌干达南部一直有着世界上最高的HIV感染率。父母往往在孩子最需要他们时候死于艾滋病，通常一个彼此依靠、互相帮助的家庭是他们的新归宿。幸运的是，这个农业区有着充足的食物，但是谁来支付他们的学费，提供医疗救助，并帮助他们成长？从长远来看，ICCF Holland希望能帮助他们照顾自己并且能照顾自己的孩子。

 

The ICCF Holland foundation

ICCF Holland 基金会

We are a small organisation, run by volunteers. 99% of the money we receive is send to the project in Uganda. Our goal is to help the most needy children, from baby to adult. The work includes improving the health situation, running a primary and secondary school and support the boys and girls finish a professional education.

我们是一个小组织，由志愿者运营管理。我们所收到的钱，99%都用于在乌干达的项目。我们的目标是帮助最需要帮助的孩子，从婴儿到成人。这项工作包括改善健康状况，运转一个小学和中学，并支持孩子们完成专业教育。

 

By concentrating on one single project we know the situation and directly talk to the staff there. If you want to sponsor a child we can discuss what boy or girl will fit you best.

通过专注于单一的项目，我们了解了情况，并直接与当地的工作人员交流。如果你想资助一个孩子，我们可以讨论哪个孩子最适合。

Christmas gifts are different this year: A gift for every child!

今年的圣诞节礼物将有所不同：每个孩子都将获得一份礼物！


Previous years only some of the children would get a special gift, if their sponsor made an extra donation. Thus disappointing all the other children. This time all the donations were put together to be able to give ALL the children a basket with useful and fun items.

前一年只有资助人提供了额外资助的孩子得到了特别的礼物，这使得别的孩子感到失望。而这一次，捐助会被汇集起来用于为所有的孩子提供一篮子有用且有趣的礼物。
