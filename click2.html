Seven Habits of Highly Effective People Seven Years of Highly Defective People

The Seven Habits book by Steven Covey is a very good book about working effectively.

I can recommend to everybody who thinks he is wasting time, is struggling with his daily life or wants to improve the effectiveness. Also applies to family life, finding the balance between work and free time.

You can find the Seven Habits book at the sites below. Buying through these links will send up to 7.5% of the sales to ICCF Holland. The money is used to help orphans in Uganda (see the home page). You should also be able to get the book in other (on-line) bookstores.

Another book that is useful when working with Vim, or rather when NOT working with Vim, is the Dilbert book Seven years of highly defective people. Very amusing. The title is a parody on the seven habits book above. The contents is an overview of the first seven years of Dilbert. It explains about the characters that appear around Dilbert and has very interesting remarks about why they were drawn this way.
