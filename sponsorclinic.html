Sponsor a room of the clinic

The clinic of the Kibaale Childrens Centre saves lives! It is the only place in and around Kibaale where people can go for proper treatment.

The number of patients has been growing steadily over the past years. The current clinic was built in 2008 and opened by the wife of president Museveni in August. This big event gave the clinic even more attention and the number of patients has grown even further. We struggle to keep providing this essential service to the community. See this page for more information about the clinic.
